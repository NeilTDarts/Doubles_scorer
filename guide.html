Score Left — the big numberThis is what you call out to the opposing marker. Both sides should match. If they don't, DIFFER shows in red.
2
✓ MATCH / DIFFERGreen MATCH means both markers agree on the score left. Red DIFFER means something doesn't add up — find the error before confirming.
3
EDIT buttonTap EDIT next to any score that is wrong. Type the correct score and tap SAVE ✓. The totals update instantly.
4
CONFIRM ROUND ✓Only tap this once everything matches. The match continues to the next round.
If you tap CANCEL the round check closes without saving any edits. The scores on the board are unchanged.
